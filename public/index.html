About Stephanie Sharp - Austin Real Estate Expert

A longtime Austin resident, Stephanie brings over 30 years of local knowledge and a diverse career spanning education, sales, and real estate. After earning her Master’s in Education from The University of Nebraska, she began her career as a teacher before moving into insurance sales and later medical devices—experiences that strengthened her ability to connect with people and understand their goals. For more than two decades, Stephanie has focused on helping buyers and sellers navigate Central Texas real estate with confidence. With over $100 million in successful transactions, she is known for her integrity, attention to detail, and client-first approach, providing expert guidance tailored to each individual’s needs. Stephanie is also a dedicated real estate investor, having renovated, flipped, and built multiple properties over the past 11 years. This hands-on experience gives her clients invaluable insight into construction, design, and market trends. Recognized for her concierge-level service, Stephanie and her skilled support team ensure every transaction is smooth, precise, and focused on achieving her clients’ goals. Outside of real estate, she enjoys traveling, making candles, and spending quality time with her daughter and dogs.
